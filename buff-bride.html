The Bridal Cut

Luxury NYC bridal wellness program that includes personalized nutrition guidance, individual personal training sessions and custom meal delivery designed to help you look and feel your best on your wedding day.

About The Bridal Cut

The Bridal Cut is a results driven, luxury bridal wellness program designed to help you achieve your wedding day goals with confidence. Whether you’re aiming to tone your arms, strengthen your back, define your abs or sculpt your entire body, your program is fully customized around your goals, timeline and current fitness level.

We combine personalized fitness, expert nutrition advice, and chef-prepared meals into a results-driven program designed to help you look and feel your best for your wedding day.

Our Integrated Approach

Signature Personal Training

Your program includes expert led private training with Guy Codio and the Elite Shape Personal Training team, fully-customized to your goals. Whether you’re aiming for sculpted arms, a stronger back, improved posture, or full-body toning, every session is intentionally designed to help you look and feel your absolute best. We combine measurable progress tracking through data-driven metrics with ongoing accountability and expert guidance to help you achieve your ideal wedding-day physique.

Personalized Nutrition CoacPETER

Work one-on-one with Registered Dietitian and Owner Danielle Falchiere and the One Nutrition Group team to create a nutrition plan tailored to your body, goals and lifestyle. This expert-led program includes private coacPETER, guidance on hormones, gut health, sleep and stress. We also provide ongoing support for travel, event planning, habits and accountability so your nutrition perfectly complements your fitness program.

Custom Meals by Square Fare

Enjoy freshly-prepared meals designed specifically for your body and goals. Every meal is individually-tailored to your calorie and macronutrient needs, aligned with your nutrition plan, built around your preferences and dietary restrictions, and delivered fresh to your door. Losing weight and/or getting fit while eating well has never been this effortless or delicious.
